About Us

Onaara Today Online College of Education is set to reset the standard of education by producing well tutored online teachers to manage the schools we are bringing online. Nothing is stopping us from becoming the Mother of the Modern Education in Nigeria, while E-School is the Father.

In 2010, we ventured into a nursery school and we named it Angel Nursery and Primary School. Our head teacher was too young to understand the passion of digitally maximizing the potential of our students by utilizing the Facebook Group as a classroom for students who love to watch things on screen to learn faster. The Covid-19 lockdown awoken the rebirth of the digital classrooms in us, and we came back to life! The need to help distant learners finally empowered the idea while Bournvita sealed the deal. Our son, the Prince was withdrawn from all physical schooling when we came about digital laboratory. Our only doubt about online education was the difficulty we had in finding teachers to handle the online schools as we would like to This brought about the idea of Teaching The Teachers! Today, we are now at the verge of revitalizing the dilapidated system of education where much energies are dissipated on prevention of examination malpractices. In online schooling, no place to hide. You either learn or leave the school!
